# Frontiers in Immunology Submission Guide

## Journal profile
- Primary audience: Immunology researchers across basic and translational work
- Editorial focus: Methodologically clear immunology studies with coherent mechanistic narrative
- Best use case: Submit when assay design and mechanistic interpretation are transparent.

## What editors usually screen first
1. Experimental control quality
2. Mechanistic claim credibility
3. Quantification and analysis transparency
4. Figure clarity for immune phenotype claims

## Practical pre-submission checks
- Document controls and gating logic clearly
- Scale mechanism claims to evidence level
- Provide reproducible quantification details
- Verify references for pathway-level claims

## Common avoidable rejection patterns
- Assay controls are present but poorly documented
- Mechanistic claims overreach from associative results
- Quantification methods are hard to reproduce

## Submission package checklist
- Control and gating documentation pass
- Mechanistic claim scope audit
- Quantification and statistics walkthrough
