# Case Study: Reducing Desk Reject Risk

## Scenario
A manuscript draft has broad claims and weak figure narrative alignment.

## Intervention
Team ran four-gate pre-submit system:
1. scope and audience check
2. claim-to-evidence map
3. reference integrity pass
4. discussion boundary rewrite

## Result
- title and abstract aligned with evidence
- figure story became easier to screen
- submission package quality improved before first submit
