# How to Reduce Desk Reject Risk

- Clarify novelty in first paragraphs
- Tighten title and abstract alignment
- Ensure methods support core claims
- Simplify figure story


## Practical sequence
1. Confirm target journal fit
2. Run claim-to-evidence map
3. Check references tied to core claims
4. Final pass on methods and limitations
