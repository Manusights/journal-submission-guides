# Desk Reject Prevention System

Most desk rejects are avoidable with a structured pre-submission system.

## Four-point prevention model
1. Scope fit is explicit in title and abstract
2. Novelty claim is realistic and evidence-backed
3. Methods depth supports every major claim
4. Figure story is legible in one pass

## Pre-submit gate workflow

### Gate 1: Scope and audience
Write one line explaining why this paper belongs in the selected journal.

### Gate 2: Claim-to-evidence map
Map every headline claim to an exact figure panel or table.

### Gate 3: Reference integrity
Check high-impact references for DOI, title, and claim support.

### Gate 4: Discussion boundaries
Ensure each major conclusion includes boundary language.

## Team operating rhythm
- Author 1: claim map owner
- Author 2: methods and stats quality owner
- Author 3: references and figure clarity owner

Run one 30-minute pre-submit review with this role split.
