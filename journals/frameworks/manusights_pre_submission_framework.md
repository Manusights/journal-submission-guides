# Manusights Pre-Submission Framework

A practical pre-submission framework used to reduce avoidable rejection.

## Step 1: Fit
- Is the manuscript aligned with journal scope and audience?
- Does the contribution clear the novelty bar for that journal tier?

## Step 2: Evidence
- Are controls and sample sizes sufficient for the claim level?
- Are limitations and uncertainty clearly stated?

## Step 3: Presentation
- Are figures readable and claims traceable to data?
- Is the abstract precise and numerically grounded?

## Step 4: Risk scan
- Citation verification
- Statistical consistency checks
- Reproducibility checklist
